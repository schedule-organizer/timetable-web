# Epic 2 - Institution Setup & Configuration

**Goal:** Allow each institution to model its own terminology, academic structure, and scheduling rules without adapting to hardcoded assumptions.

**Primary personas:** Timetabler, Principal

**Business value:**
- delivers one of SchediFlow's strongest differentiators: configurability
- lowers onboarding friction for varied institution types
- supports expansion beyond one school model or geography

**Key capabilities:**
- terminology and label customization
- difficulty scale and room-type configuration
- academic year and term management
- bell schedule creation and editing
- holiday and vacation calendar management

**Frontend alignment:** `CONFIG-06` to `CONFIG-08`, `HOL-06`

**Success signal:** a school can fully define its scheduling vocabulary, calendar, and operating structure before creating resources.
