# Epic 10 - Reporting, Auditability, and Operational Insight

**Goal:** Give leadership and timetablers evidence that schedules are fair, efficient, and governable.

**Primary personas:** Principal, Timetabler, Admin

**Business value:**
- supports trust, oversight, and operational improvement
- reinforces the product's positioning as transparent rather than opaque
- creates administrative value beyond timetable generation

**Key capabilities:**
- PDF, CSV, and iCal export
- teacher, room, and subject coverage reporting
- audit log visibility for changes and approvals

**Frontend alignment:** `EXPORT-04` to `EXPORT-08`

**Success signal:** leaders can review utilization and change history without manually assembling reports from multiple tools.
