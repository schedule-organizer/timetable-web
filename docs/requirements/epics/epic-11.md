# Epic 11 - AI Scheduling Assistant (Post-MVP)

**Goal:** Introduce natural-language and AI-assisted scheduling workflows once the core scheduling model is stable.

**Primary personas:** Timetabler, Principal

**Business value:**
- advances a major market differentiator from the MRD
- lowers expertise requirements even further
- creates a strong launchable Phase 2 story for market expansion

**Key capabilities:**
- natural-language setup assistance
- AI help for schedule generation and issue resolution
- guided recommendations when hard or soft constraints conflict

**Frontend alignment:** not yet defined in `schediflow-frontend_Architecture_v1.2`

**Success signal:** users can express scheduling intent in plain language and receive useful, trustworthy assistance.
