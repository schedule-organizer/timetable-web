# Epic 5 - Timetable Workspace & Multi-View Navigation

**Goal:** Provide the central operational workspace where schedules can be viewed from the perspective of different stakeholders and resources.

**Primary personas:** Timetabler, Teacher, Student, Principal

**Business value:**
- makes the timetable understandable and actionable
- supports institution-wide access rather than admin-only usage
- creates the main product surface users return to every day

**Key capabilities:**
- timetable grid experience
- class, teacher, room, subject, student, and master views
- lesson cards with state indicators for conflicts, pins, and cover

**Frontend alignment:** `SCHED-12`, `SCHED-13`, `SCHED-14`

**Success signal:** users can reliably inspect the current timetable from the view most relevant to them.
