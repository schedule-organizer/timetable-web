# Epic 6 - Scheduling Engine Control, Quality, and Manual Adjustment

**Goal:** Turn timetable generation into a guided, transparent workflow rather than a black-box batch process.

**Primary personas:** Timetabler, Principal

**Business value:**
- delivers the core promise of intelligent scheduling
- supports SchediFlow's sensitivity-control differentiation
- reduces fear and frustration when schedules need iteration

**Key capabilities:**
- run and cancel scheduling jobs
- sensitivity dial and constraint weights
- progress visibility and quality score explanation
- conflict detail surfacing
- drag-and-drop lesson adjustment with optimistic updates
- pin and swap actions for manual refinement

**Frontend alignment:** `SCHED-12` to `SCHED-18`

**Success signal:** a timetabler can generate a schedule, understand quality tradeoffs, and make guided adjustments without breaking trust in the system.
