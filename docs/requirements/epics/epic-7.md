# Epic 7 - Publishing & Stakeholder Schedule Access

**Goal:** Move from draft schedule creation to institution-wide schedule consumption.

**Primary personas:** Teacher, Student, Parent, Principal

**Business value:**
- converts internal schedule work into visible end-user value
- increases stickiness through whole-community adoption
- supports reliable communication after timetable publication

**Key capabilities:**
- publish timetable workflow
- personal timetable access across roles
- exported and shareable schedule formats
- stakeholder-friendly access patterns on responsive web

**Frontend alignment:** `EXPORT-04` and timetable-view stories in `SCHED-12` to `SCHED-14`

**Success signal:** once published, the right people can access the right timetable view without depending on paper distribution.
