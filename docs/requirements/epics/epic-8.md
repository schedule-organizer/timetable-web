# Epic 8 - Daily Operations: Cover, Delegation, and Temporary Schedules

**Goal:** Support the real operational life of a school after the master timetable exists.

**Primary personas:** Teacher, Timetabler, Moderator, Principal

**Business value:**
- addresses one of the clearest market gaps identified in the MRD
- extends product value beyond once-per-term planning
- improves retention through daily workflow relevance

**Key capabilities:**
- teacher absence handling and cover assignment
- delegation request and approval workflow
- temporary schedules for exam weeks, trips, and special events
- visible schedule-state messaging when temporary schedules are active

**Frontend alignment:** `COVER-08` to `COVER-11`

**Success signal:** institutions can handle day-of and short-term changes inside the platform rather than through ad hoc calls or chat threads.
