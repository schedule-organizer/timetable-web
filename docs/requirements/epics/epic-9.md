# Epic 9 - Notifications & Preference Management

**Goal:** Ensure important schedule changes reach the right people through structured, configurable communication.

**Primary personas:** Teacher, Student, Parent, Moderator

**Business value:**
- reduces missed schedule changes and operational confusion
- increases confidence that the platform is the source of truth
- supports community-wide usage across device types

**Key capabilities:**
- in-app notification center
- unread status and event-based alerts
- per-user notification preferences by event and channel

**Frontend alignment:** `NOTIF-01`, `NOTIF-04`

**Success signal:** users are informed about changes that affect them and can control the level of communication they receive.
